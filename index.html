Compassionate Service Society

Project OverView
Compassionate Service Society is a non profit organization based on buddhist principles, that offer a variety of programs, that seek to unfold the light of wisdom and compassion innate in all of us.
Our goal was to redesign their website to provide our users with a straightforward and efficient path to discover programs and connect with the community.
User Research
When watching our users navigate the website, given specific tasks, we gained a lot of insight with the natural flow a user would take and the difficulties that occurred along the way.

To evaluate this, we focused on 4 specific tasks:

  • 1. Find the schedule for Tai Chi sessions
  • 2. Find information on an upcoming vegetarian class
  • 3. Find the Meditation Student Login Page
  • 4. Register for an event of your choice
Affinity Diagram & Key Findings

As a team we evaluated our user interview findings and created an affinity diagram to further analyze key findings.

Problem Statement

The Compassionate Service Society website aims to inform visitors about the organization and inspire them to get involved in their initiatives.

We have observed that the current navigation system is confusing and prevents users from easily accessing information about upcoming events and programs. (This hinders their ability to successfully engage with the organization.)

How might we improve the navigation system to make their website easier to locate critical information about programs and events?

User Testing


Based on our redesign, we then conducted 8 more user tests to check fluidity and if our users display signs of intuitive navigating.

To evaluate this, we focused on these 4 specific tasks:
1. Find the about page
2. Find information on an upcoming vegetarian class
3. Find the Meditation Student Login Page
4. Register for an event of your choice
Hi Fidelity Iterations
From the feedback received in our user testing, we iterated on both desktop and mobile wireframes, in addition to implementing our style guide.

Iterating the hamburger menu increased the functionality and overall flow of our web design.
